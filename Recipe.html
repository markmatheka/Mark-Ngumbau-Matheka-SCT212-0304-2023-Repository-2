Biryani Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
  • 2-3 green chilies, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (optional)
  • 1/4 cup cooking oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2-3 cloves
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak the Basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat the cooking oil over medium heat.
  3. Add the cumin seeds, cloves, cardamom, and cinnamon. Sauté for a minute until they sizzle and release their aroma.
  4. Add the sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown.
  5. Stir in the minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped green chilies. Sauté for a couple of minutes.
  6. If using mixed vegetables, add them at this stage and cook for a few minutes.
  7. Now, add the soaked and drained rice to the pot and sauté for 2-3 minutes, stirring gently.
  8. Pour in the 4 cups of water, add turmeric powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  9. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for about 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and the water is absorbed.
  10. Once done, fluff the rice with a fork, garnish with fresh cilantro leaves, and serve your delicious biryani rice!

Enjoy your homemade biryani rice!
